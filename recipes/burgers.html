Hamburgers

An American Classic!

No matter the occasion you can't go wrong with hamburgers!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Quarter Stick of Butter
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Patty the ground beef into whatever sized burger you want
  2. Season the patty with salt and pepper and any other seasoning you prefer
  3. Use butter on a skillet with medium heat
  4. Make a cut into the middle of the patty to see the inside of the patty
  5. Cook the patty until desired pinkness
  6. Put the patty on a bun and enjoy
