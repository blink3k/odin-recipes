Pancakes

The best breakfast!

You won't be disappointed with this recipe, it's a classic! It's an extremely simple recipe that will have you coming back to make more!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of pancake mix
  • 1 egg
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 2 tbsp of cooking oil

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Preheat skillet to 350 degrees
  3. Mix ingredients together
  4. Let mix thicken for three to five minutes
  5. Pour mix onto skillet and cook a minute and a half on each side
