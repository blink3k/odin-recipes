Potato Stroganoff

A great dinner for any occasion!

Nothing beats home made potato stroganoff! It's my personal favorite dish that I'm happy to share with you.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Helper
  • 1 Pound of Ground Beef
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 2 Cups of Hot Water

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef until there's no pink left
  2. Strain the meat and add back into pan
  3. Add the milk, hot water, seasoning packet, and potatoes to the pan until boiling
  4. Once boiling reduce heat and let simmer for 25 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
